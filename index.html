BU SAIF HUMANOID ROBOT — GITEX 2019 · DHA CONTEXT
Project Brief — Bu Saif
An interactive humanoid robot I designed and developed at UAE University, later showcased at GITEX 2019 and extended into a senior-care cognitive-assessment context.
This work also extended into healthcare-facing use cases. In April 2021, Al Khaleej reported that Bu Saif itself was used through a Dubai senior-services context to help assess the cognitive abilities of senior citizens.
